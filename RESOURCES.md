# TATVA — Geospatial Resource Index

## Cities, Towns, Locations & Labels

### Place Name Databases
- [GeoNames](https://www.geonames.org) — 21M+ place names, free
- [GeoNames Data Download](https://download.geonames.org/export/dump/)
- [OSMNames](https://osmnames.org) — 21M streets, cities, towns, open source
- [OSMNames GitHub](https://github.com/OSMNames/OSMNames) — Downloadable TSV
- [Geoapify Free City/Town/Village Dataset](https://www.geoapify.com/download-all-the-cities-towns-villages/)
- [CountryStateCity API](https://countrystatecity.in) — 250+ countries, 151K+ cities
- [GeoDB Cities API](http://geodb-cities-api.wirefreethought.com) — GraphQL + REST

### Geocoding
- [Nominatim / OpenStreetMap Geocoder](https://nominatim.org)
- [OpenStreetMap Tile Server](https://tile.openstreetmap.org)

### Country & Border Data
- [Natural Earth](https://www.naturalearthdata.com) — Free vector boundaries, borders, labels
- [OpenStreetMap Boundaries](https://osm-boundaries.com)

## Weather Patterns (Clouds, Storms, Wind, Rain)

### Weather APIs
- [Open-Meteo](https://open-meteo.com) — Free, no key needed, wind/temp/rain/clouds
- [Open-Meteo GitHub](https://github.com/open-meteo/open-meteo)
- [OpenWeatherMap API](https://openweathermap.org/api) — Clouds, storms, precipitation

### Weather Visualization Libraries (WebGL)
- [Fluid Earth](https://github.com/byrd-polar/fluid-earth) — Open source WebGL weather globe
- [Mapbox WebGL Wind](https://github.com/mapbox/webgl-wind) — 1M wind particles at 60fps
- [WeatherLayers](https://weatherlayers.com) — WebGL 2, globe support
- [MapTiler Weather](https://www.maptiler.com/weather/) — WebGL animated wind/clouds

### Storm & Severe Weather
- [NOAA GFS](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/weather-climate-models/global-forecast) — Global Forecast System, free
- [NOAA Real-Time Weather Data](https://www.weather.gov/documentation/services-web-api)

## NASA Satellite Imagery
- [NASA GIBS API](https://nasa-gibs.github.io/gibs-api-docs/) — 1000+ satellite layers, near real-time
- [NASA Worldview](https://worldview.earthdata.nasa.gov) — Interactive satellite imagery browser
- [NASA Worldview GitHub](https://github.com/nasa-gibs/worldview)
- [NASA GIBS WMS Endpoint](https://gibs.earthdata.nasa.gov/wms/epsg4326/best/wms.cgi)
- [NASA Earthdata Portal](https://www.earthdata.nasa.gov)

## Terrain & Elevation
- [OpenTopography API](https://opentopography.org/developers) — LiDAR, SRTM, 30m global
- [USGS Elevation Data](https://apps.nationalmap.gov/downloader/)
- [NASA SRTM](https://www2.jpl.nasa.gov/srtm/) — 30m global elevation
- [Copernicus DEM](https://spacedata.copernicus.eu/collections/copernicus-digital-elevation-model) — 30m global, ESA
- [JAXA ALOS World 3D](https://www.eorc.jaxa.jp/ALOS/en/dataset/aw3d30/aw3d30_e.htm) — Most precise global DEM
- [MapTiler Terrain](https://www.maptiler.com/terrain/) — 3D tiles for CesiumJS
- [MapLibre 3D Terrain](https://maplibre.org/maplibre-gl-js/docs/examples/3d-terrain/)

## Ocean & Bathymetry
- [GEBCO](https://www.gebco.net) — General Bathymetric Chart of the Oceans, free
- [NOAA Ocean Data](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/products/ocean-data)

## Space & Starfield
- [NASA SVS Deep Star Maps 2020](https://svs.gsfc.nasa.gov/4851) — 1.7B stars from Hipparcos-2, Tycho-2, Gaia DR2; equirectangular textures
- [NASA API](https://api.nasa.gov) — APOD, DONKI (space weather), EPIC, etc.
- [What color is the Sun?](http://www.vendian.org/mncharity/dir3/starcolor/sun.html) — CIE chromaticity above atmosphere (#fff3ea)

## Globe Frameworks
- [CesiumJS](https://cesium.com/cesiumjs/) — Terrain, imagery, labels, weather
- [NASA WorldWind](https://worldwind.arc.nasa.gov) — Open source globe SDK
- [MapLibre GL JS](https://maplibre.org) — Open source WebGL maps/globe
